We can only see a short distance ahead, but we can see plenty there that needs to be done. - Alan Turing

Hello, I am Carlton Smith! I am a senior undergraduate physics and math student at the University of Florida. A bit of background, I entered university with an interest in planetary science and astronomy, allured by the opportunity to contribute to our mapping of 'the final frontier'. Steadily, as time has progressed I've grown interested in the physics of complex systems, especially those found in living matter. For later portion of my undergraduate, I've had the privilege of working in research experiences related to deep learning, image denoising, machine learning, and neuroscinece. My research interests include theoretical and computational biophysics and neuroscience - specifically, applications of statsitical physics to studying complex biological systems.

In my free time you can catch me listening to music, practicing my bass or guitar, reading, or exploring cityscapes!
