Hello, I am Carlton Smith! I am a senior undergraduate physics student at the University of Florida. I've had the privilege of working in research experiences related to deep learning, image denoising, machine learning, and neuroscinece. My research interests include theoretical and computational biophysics and neuroscience - specifically, applications of statsitical physics to studying complex biological systems.

In my free time you can catch me listening or practicing music (I love bossa nova, jazz, funk, and folk), consuming science fiction media, and exploring cityscapes!
